Flux Relay is a compact logic puzzle game about shaping magnetic fields inside a sci-fi lab.

Move magnets, pulse the grid, and guide charged glass blocks into matching sockets. Every level is deterministic: victory comes from reading polarity, line of sight, blockers, hazards, and pulse timing.

Features:
- Portrait iPhone puzzle play for short sessions.
- Hand-built onboarding levels and a broad campaign of generated lab grids.
- Cyan, amber, and violet polarity logic.
- Tactile SpriteKit motion, haptics, and a premium lab-style completion handoff.
- Local progress with no account, no tracking, and no ads in this first release.

Flux Relay is built for players who like clean rules, clever grids, and satisfying magnetic chain reactions.
