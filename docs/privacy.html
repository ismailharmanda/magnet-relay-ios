Flux Relay Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 21, 2026

Flux Relay does not collect personal data, does not track users, and does not use third-party analytics or advertising SDKs.

Data Stored On Device

The app stores local game progress, hint credits, and settings on your device so the game can remember completed levels. This data is not sent to us.

Network, Accounts, and Purchases

Flux Relay does not require an account, does not include in-app purchases in this release, and does not send gameplay data to a server.

Contact

For privacy questions, email iharmanda@hotmail.com.
