# Tabler Icons Test Art

The board glyphs in this asset catalog are local test art derived from Tabler Icons.

- Source: https://tabler.io/icons
- Repository: https://github.com/tabler/tabler-icons
- License: MIT
- Icons used: magnet, target, broadcast, barrier-block, shield-bolt, circuit-battery

These icons are acceptable for local prototype/testing use. Before production release, replace them with owned/commissioned art or explicitly approve third-party icon usage for release.
